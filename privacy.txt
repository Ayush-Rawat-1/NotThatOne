Privacy Policy for NotThatOne

NotThatOne values your privacy. This extension is designed to function entirely client-side on your local device. 

1. Data We Collect:
- Personally Identifiable Information: The extension captures and stores the user's Google account email address selected during login processes.
- User Activity: The extension interacts with specific page click elements on accounts.google.com to register which email profile was selected.

2. How We Use and Store Data:
All captured data is saved strictly using the local web browser storage API (chrome.storage.local). This configuration data stays entirely within your local machine's sandbox environment. 

3. Third-Party Sharing:
We do not host remote servers. We have zero access to your information. Your data is never sold, transmitted, or shared with any third party, analytics providers, or external cloud systems.

For questions, please open an issue on the project's official repository.
